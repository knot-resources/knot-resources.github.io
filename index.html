Customer Story: Unboxed

Unboxed is an SME with 50 employees, based in London and Cape Town.

After a financially turbulent couple of years, Unboxed was suffering with disgruntled people reacting to a negative atmosphere. This negativity led to non-constructive feedback, very little growth and unhappy people.

Enter Knot

Within a year Knot helped Unboxed to halve attrition rates and moved the organisation to an employee-empowered, people-led, happy environment that our excellent people thrive in. Today, Unboxed have processes and systems in place which are embedded in their daily culture.


“We know that people are our value but, before Knot, we did not necessarily show them that. Our efforts were often not recognised and it left leadership feeling unappreciated and our employees feeling that they were unable to live our values.”

Richard Stobart, CEO, Unboxed
