How we do it

Getting new people embedded as efficiently as possible both empowers them and maximises the return on your investment in them. It sets them on your cultural path and facilitates productive, competent contributions to delivering on your strategy.

We centre the candidate filtering process around candidates in recruitment.

Our innovative self selection tool encourages candidates to opt into a conversation, reduces the amount of time spent speaking to the wrong candidate and eliminates unsuitable candidates early in the process.

After opting into a conversation, passing technical and behavioural tests and accepting an offer, new joiners are issued with our onboarding game.

Centred around the new joiner’s experience of induction, our onboarding game facilitates interaction with key business people in their early days, is simplified so that business critical operations are covered and makes learning about the organisation fun.

The culmination of this primes your new joiner to deliver value from day 1...
